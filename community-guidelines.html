BeThere Club

Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 12, 2026

BeThere Club exists to help people form real, meaningful, platonic connections. These Community Guidelines set the expectations for how every member — seeker or companion — should behave on our platform and during meetups. By using BeThere, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Our golden rule: Treat every person on BeThere the way you'd want to be treated — with respect, honesty, and kindness.

1. Platonic Only — No Exceptions

BeThere Club is built exclusively for platonic connections. This is the foundation of everything we do.

  • Do not use BeThere to seek romantic or sexual encounters.
  • Do not send flirtatious, suggestive, or sexually explicit messages.
  • Do not request or offer personal services outside the scope of platonic companionship.
  • Do not use the platform as a dating service, escort service, or matchmaking tool.

Any attempt to use BeThere for non-platonic purposes will result in immediate and permanent account termination.

2. Respect Everyone

Every person on BeThere deserves to feel safe and respected. This means:

  • No harassment, bullying, intimidation, or threatening behaviour — online or during meetups.
  • No hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language based on race, religion, caste, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic.
  • No body-shaming, personal attacks, or demeaning comments.
  • No unsolicited advice or pressure on personal choices.

Disagreements happen. Handle them with maturity. If a conversation or meetup becomes uncomfortable, you are always free to end it politely and report the issue.

3. Be Honest

Trust is the backbone of our community. Every member must:

  • Use their real name and a recent, accurate photo.
  • Provide truthful information in their profile and bio.
  • Complete identity verification (KYC) honestly — using someone else's ID is strictly prohibited.
  • Not misrepresent their skills, interests, or availability as a companion.
  • Not create fake accounts, duplicate accounts, or impersonate another person.

4. Keep Meetups Safe

Safety is non-negotiable. Every meetup must follow these rules:

  • Public venues only: All meetups must happen at BeThere-verified public venues — cafes, libraries, parks, co-working spaces, etc. Never agree to meet at a private residence.
  • Check in via the app: Both the seeker and companion must check in at the venue using the app's selfie verification feature.
  • Stay in public areas: Do not move the meetup to a private or secluded location.
  • No alcohol or substance pressure: Never pressure your meetup partner to drink alcohol or consume any substance. Respect their choices.
  • Leave if uncomfortable: If at any point you feel unsafe, you have every right to leave immediately. Report the incident through the app.

5. Protect Privacy

Respect the privacy of every BeThere member:

  • Do not share another user's personal information (phone number, address, workplace, social media) without their explicit consent.
  • Do not screenshot or share private in-app conversations outside the platform.
  • Do not take or post photos/videos of your meetup partner without their permission.
  • Keep all communication within the BeThere app until both parties mutually agree to share contact details.
  • Do not stalk, track, or attempt to contact a user through channels outside the platform after they have declined communication.

6. Fair Reviews

Reviews help our community thrive. When leaving a review:

  • Be honest and constructive — share your genuine experience.
  • Focus on the meetup experience, not personal characteristics or appearance.
  • Do not leave fake reviews — whether positive (to boost your own profile) or negative (to harm someone else).
  • Do not use reviews to harass, threaten, or retaliate against another user.
  • Do not offer or accept payment in exchange for reviews.

7. No Commercial Activity

BeThere is for genuine human connection, not business promotion:

  • Do not use meetups to sell products, services, or recruit for MLM schemes.
  • Do not solicit donations or fundraise during meetups.
  • Do not distribute promotional material or advertise businesses through your profile or messages.
  • Do not attempt to bypass the platform's payment system by arranging off-platform payments with seekers or companions.

8. No Illegal Activity

You must not use BeThere Club for any activity that violates Indian law or the laws of the jurisdiction you are in. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Sale, purchase, or use of illegal substances during meetups.
  • Theft, fraud, or financial scams targeting other users.
  • Distributing or sharing illegal content through the platform.
  • Using the platform to plan or coordinate any criminal activity.

9. Companion Standards

If you are a companion on BeThere, you are the face of our community. We hold companions to a higher standard:

  • Be punctual: Arrive on time for confirmed bookings. Chronic lateness affects your rating and may result in suspension.
  • Be present: Give your seeker your full attention. Avoid spending the meetup on your phone or being disengaged.
  • Be professional: Maintain a friendly, warm, and professional demeanor throughout the meetup.
  • Communicate clearly: If you need to cancel, do so as early as possible through the app. Repeated last-minute cancellations or no-shows will result in account suspension.
  • Set clear boundaries: You have every right to set boundaries about the nature of the meetup, topics of conversation, and duration. Communicate them clearly and respectfully.

10. Seeker Expectations

If you are a seeker on BeThere, here's what we expect from you:

  • Be respectful of your companion's time: Show up on time and stay for the booked duration.
  • Respect boundaries: If your companion sets a boundary, honour it without argument.
  • No pressure: Do not pressure a companion to extend the meetup, meet off-platform, or do anything outside the agreed terms.
  • Pay through the platform: All payments must go through BeThere's escrow system. Never ask a companion to accept direct payment.

11. Reporting & Enforcement

If you witness or experience a violation of these guidelines, report it immediately:

  • In-app reporting: Use the safety reporting feature available on every profile and during every booking.
  • Email: Contact support@bethere.club with your booking ID and a description of the incident.
  • Emergency: If you are in immediate physical danger, contact local law enforcement first, then report the incident to us.

All reports are reviewed by our Trust & Safety team. We take every report seriously and will investigate promptly. Outcomes may include:

  • A warning to the offending user
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Cooperation with law enforcement where required

Filing false or malicious reports is itself a violation of these guidelines and may result in action against the reporting user.

12. Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines as our platform evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use of BeThere after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised guidelines.
