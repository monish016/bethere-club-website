Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Teckas Technologies ("we," "us," or "BeThere Club") operates the BeThere Club platform (website at bethere.club and mobile application). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

By using BeThere Club, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, city, and profile photo.

Identity Verification (KYC): To maintain safety, we collect your Aadhaar number (or other government-issued ID) through our secure third-party verification partner. This data is used solely for identity verification and is handled in compliance with the Aadhaar Act, 2016 and UIDAI guidelines. We do not store your full Aadhaar number; only the verification status is retained.

Profile Information: Bio, interests, companion categories, availability, hourly rates (for companions), and profile preferences you choose to share.

Booking & Transaction Data: Booking details (date, time, venue, companion selected), payment amounts, escrow transaction records, and review/rating data.

Usage Data: Device information (device type, operating system, browser type), IP address, app usage patterns, pages visited, and interaction timestamps.

Location Data: Approximate location based on your city selection. We do not track precise GPS location unless you explicitly enable it for venue-based features.

Communications: Messages exchanged through the in-app chat system between seekers and companions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and profile
  • To verify your identity through KYC and maintain platform safety
  • To facilitate companion discovery, booking, and meetup coordination
  • To process payments through our escrow system and pay companions
  • To display ratings and reviews to other users
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and service notifications
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations
  • To improve the platform, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your public profile (name, photo, bio, ratings, categories) is visible to other BeThere users. Your phone number and email are never shared with other users.
  • KYC Verification Partners: Government ID data is shared with our authorized verification provider solely for identity verification.
  • Payment Processors: Transaction data is shared with our payment gateway provider to process bookings and payouts.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users, including cooperation with law enforcement in case of reported safety incidents.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services for hosting, analytics, email delivery, and push notifications. These providers process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL), secure database access controls, and regular security audits. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (such as transaction records for tax compliance).

5. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing (this may affect your ability to use the platform)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bethere.club.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

BeThere Club is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will terminate their account and delete their data promptly.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
