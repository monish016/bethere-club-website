Questions

Frequently Asked

Is BeThere a dating app? +
Absolutely not. BeThere is designed exclusively for platonic connections. Every feature, from companion categories to venue selection, is built to foster friendship, mentoring, and social companionship. No dating. No ambiguity.
How are companions verified? +
Every companion undergoes KYC verification with a government-issued ID before they can be listed on the platform. We also require selfie verification during meetups and maintain a review-based trust score system.
What happens if someone doesn't show up? +
We have a 100% no-show penalty policy. Payments are held in escrow until both parties check in at the venue. If someone doesn't show, you get a full refund and the no-show gets flagged on their profile.
How much does it cost? +
Companion rates start from ₹100/hr and vary by category and experience. BeThere charges a flat 7% platform fee. All prices are shown upfront before you book. No hidden charges, ever.
Which cities is BeThere available in? +
We're currently in beta and launching in Chennai and Bangalore first. We're rapidly expanding to more Indian cities. Sign up to be notified when we're in your city.
Can I become a companion? +
Yes! You can switch between Seeker and Companion roles right from your profile. Complete your KYC verification, set your categories and rates, and start accepting bookings. It's a great way to earn while making genuine connections.
